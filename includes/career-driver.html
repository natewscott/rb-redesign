Class A Driver

A Class A Driver will work under the supervision of the warehouse manager, provide excellent customer service, support warehouse operations as needed and make jobsite customer deliveries. This postion is daily Bay Area driving, no long-haul or overnight deliveries.

You must have a current Califronia driver's license, clean DMV record, with a postive forward thinking attitude. The position also requires the ability to lift up to 50lbs. Water works or underground and jobsite delivery experience is a plus. This position is full-time (Mon-Fri) with potential for daily overtime during construction season.

What we provide:

  • Mentoring program
  • Health Benefits
  • 401K
  • Safety Incentives
