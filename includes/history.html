About Us

Established in 1949. Roberts & Brune Company was founded in 1947 by Bob Roberts on July 1, 1949 Fred Brune Joined Bob creating a partnership named Roberts and Brune Company. In 1994 Reed Mack purchased the assets of Roberts and Brune Company now known as R&B Company.

We have grown to become Northern California's largest wholesale distributor for Waterworks, Fire Protection, Irrgation, Gas and Fusible pipeline products. With 11 warehouses we have California covered from Bakersfield to Chico.
