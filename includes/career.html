About Us

When you join R&B company, it means more than just starting a new job. It means becoming a part of a family and a community that has always put people first. We encourage our associates to build lasting relationships with each and the people that we serve every day. We believe that learning is a lifelong process and encourage employees to become mentors to each other. In doing so we take risks, adapt and progress together. We are passionate about everything that we do here because at the of the day it, not just pipe, valves and fittings. We help provide the water that improves people's quality of lives.
