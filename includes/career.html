About Us

When you join R&B company, it means more than just starting a new job. It means becoming a part of a family and a community that has always put people first. We encourage our associates to build lasting relationships with each and the people that we serve every day. We believe that learning is a lifelong process and encourage employees to become mentors to each other. In doing so we take risks, adapt and progress together. There is passion in everything that we do here; because at the of the day, we are not just selling pipe, valves, and fittings. We are helping provide the water that improves people's quality of lives.
