R&B Mobile App

R&B is pleased to be the only Northern California underground utility wholesaler to make available a mobile app to assist engineers, utilities contractors, and pipefitters alike get answers to questions on a job site.

  • iTunes

  • Google Play

  • Blackberry

Instructions for installing on Blackberry:

  • 1)Log onto your Blackberry App Store. In the search bar, type in "rb mobile resources," the app will be the first one on the list. If that is too much to remember, type in "pipe fitters," and our app will appear in the top five.
  • 2)Simply click on the "Run" tab and you are off to the races with the new RB Mobile Resources. We would be thrilled if you would recommend our new app to your co-workers and customers alike.
  • 3)You can save the app on your Blackberry Smart Device (phone) in your Applications folder as illustrated here. We hope you enjoy using this user friendly application. We welcome your comments and suggestions on any additional services we can provide you when you are mobile.
